Commercial Framework

The Enterprise Billing Structure

We do not use complex recurring monthly SaaS subscriptions. We align perfectly with standard B2B industrial procurement cycles.

🏢

1. Initial Capital Outlay (Capex)

Covers physical edge server deployment, lens calibration, camera alignment, PLC integration modules, and deep learning model custom training for your floor light levels. Billed locally through direct bank transfer once contractually agreed accuracy metrics are satisfied.

🛡️

2. Annual Maintenance (AMC)

Includes 24/7 rapid zone engineering support, physical camera recalibrations, custom model tuning for new fabric overlays or packaging styles, and continuous API security patches. Billed annually starting in Year 2.

📷

3. Zero Camera CapEx

Because our core computer vision models run directly on your existing security IP cameras (Hikvision, Dahua, Axis), your initial deployment requires zero reinvestment in optical infrastructure.
