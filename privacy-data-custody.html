Local Data Custody & Security Policy

We contractually guarantee that your factory floor layout designs, line operations, and operator analytics remain securely within your physical building.

1. Local-First Air-Gapped Deploys

Scenviz Technologies software does not stream raw video channels or processing streams to cloud databases. All deep learning and computer vision visual analytics run on-site on physically secured edge servers. No internet-exposed streaming protocols are active out of the box, preventing any structural IP leaks to competitors or external systems.

2. Audit-Ready Log Storage

Compliance event timestamps, safety lane clearance verifications, and operator PPE checks are stored in a local, encrypted SQLite/PostgreSQL database on your edge node. Automated reports generated for Accord, WRAP, or BSCI compliance audits are signed with unique cryptographic hashes to guarantee data integrity to international buyers while keeping sensitive operations data localized.

3. Local Capex & Compliance Tax Alignment

Our operations are fully registered under Bangladesh corporate laws. All transactions are settled directly via local bank transfers under official VAT-compliant corporate invoices. This removes all complex currency conversions or letter of credit (L/C) delays and ensures absolute alignment with national B2B tax compliance standards, simplifying your tax filing and audit tracking.
